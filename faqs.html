Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We’ve got answers. Here’s everything you need to know about studying abroad with 3M.

Application Process

Q: How do I start my application?

A: Click the "Apply Now" button and fill out your basic info. Our advisors will guide you through the next steps.

Q: Can I apply to more than one university?

A: Yes, you can apply to multiple universities depending on your preferences.

Q: Do I need to write a personal statement or essay?

A: Most universities require it, but we will guide you through the process.

Eligibility & Requirements

Q: What grades do I need to study abroad?

A: It depends on the destination and university. A minimum GPA of 2.5+ is common, but we guide all academic levels.

Q: Do I need IELTS or TOEFL?

A: Many universities accept these exams, but some offer alternatives.

Visas & Travel

Q: Do you help with student visas?

A: 100%. We assist with documentation, interview prep, and appointment bookings.

Q: How long does the visa process take?

A: It varies by country, but we’ll guide you through the timeline.

Costs & Scholarships

Q: How much does it cost to study abroad?

A: Tuition ranges from $5,000 to $40,000/year depending on the country and program. We’ll help you budget smart.

Q: Are there scholarships available?

A: Yes, many universities and organizations offer scholarships.

After Admission

Q: What happens after I’m accepted?

A: We guide you through visa steps, pre-departure orientation, and help you settle in abroad.

Q: Can I work while studying abroad?

A: Yes, many countries allow part-time work for international students.
