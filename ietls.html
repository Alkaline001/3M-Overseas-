What is IELTS?

The International English Language Testing System (IELTS) assesses proficiency in the English language. IELTS measures English competency of individuals who want to study or work in places where English is the mode of communication.

The skillfulness in English language is determined on a nine-band scale. The scale ranges from band score 1 (nonuser) to band score 9 (expert). The exam tests four skills: Listening, Reading, Writing and Speaking skills.

Types of IELTS Tests

IELTS Academic

The IELTS Academic test is suitable for candidates applying for higher education or professional registration in English-speaking countries. It is applicable for undergraduate and postgraduate levels.

IELTS General Training

The IELTS General Training Test is for individuals migrating for work experience, training programs, or secondary education in an English-speaking environment. The test assesses survival skills in workplace and social settings.
